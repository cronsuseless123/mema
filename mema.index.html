HYPERPOEM: LEADERSHIP

Leadership is a journey, A journey where you must survive, Survive with many responsibilities, But only your faults they can see. A moment you'll speak. A moment they'll listen. An important discussion, But it turns to silence. position is just a title. A voice that is never spoken, A hand that raises corruption, And the collaboration of irresponsible persons. A few responsible leaders, It's like we're counting our fingers. No voice, no power, but everyone needs you. A chance we've grabbed, a better future a head.

By: John Wayne Buitizon
